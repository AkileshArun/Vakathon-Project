FISH AI - WEB EDITION  (quick start)
====================================
1. Double-click  fishai-web.html  to open it in your browser.
2. Create your angler profile.
3. Open the side "API Keys" tab, paste your Gemini key, and Save.
   This powers the AI coach + photo fish detection.
   (Without a key, the coach still works offline.)
4. Scan a fish photo -> it auto-fills your catch log.

Needs internet to load (uses Tailwind/Leaflet/Chart from CDN).
Everything is stored only in your browser.
