Why mercury matters here

Mercury from historic mining (like the 1800s gold rush) and coal plants settles into rivers and lakes, then climbs the food chain — so big, old, predatory fish carry the most. It can't be cooked out. This app flags it early so you keep the healthy catches and skip the risky ones. Guidance follows FDA/EPA fish-advisory tiers and is informational, not medical advice.
